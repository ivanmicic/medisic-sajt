Acacia honey – natural honey from carefully tended hives

Pure acacia honey from the MeDišić family farm. A mild taste, a light colour, and the clear mark of the acacia flow — without rush and without shortcuts.

What you get in the jar

Our acacia honey is natural honey from the acacia flow. It stays true to what the bees bring — mild, light, and pleasant for everyday use.

We work as a family farm. That is why every jar carries the same standard: care around the hives, calm work in season, and honey that does not need to be “fixed”.

Why acacia honey is special

The acacia flow gives the honey a character that is easy to recognise.

  • Mild, clean taste

    Acacia honey is known for its mild taste. With us it stays natural — without additives and without “tuning” the character.

  • Light colour

    The typical light shade of acacia honey. A clear visual sign of the forage it comes from.

  • For the table and for gifts

    Suitable for everyday use, and special enough to be a gift that speaks of quality and origin.

How it is made — natural production

The honey is made in the rhythm of the season. We follow the acacia flow, keep calm around the hives, and harvest when the honey is ready — not sooner.

Natural production for us means a short path from apiary to jar and respect for what the bees bring. Without rush. Without shortcuts.

Quality and origin

Acacia honey comes from carefully tended hives of the MeDišić family farm. The origin is clear: our apiary, our care, our jar.

We insist on natural honey because we believe quality does not need to be proven with big words — it is felt in the taste and in the trust we build order by order.
