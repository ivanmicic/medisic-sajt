Our production

Homemade fruit brandies are an additional product line of the MeDišić family farm. We work on a smaller scale, with a focus on clean fruit flavour and careful distillation.

The tone is premium, yet remains homemade: without pretence, with respect for the fruit and the time the process needs.

Quality and recognition

Our brandies have won awards at competitions in Serbia — proof that a homemade approach and patience deliver a result people recognise.

We measure quality by taste and trust. Awards are an important mark — but they do not replace the care we put into every distillation.
