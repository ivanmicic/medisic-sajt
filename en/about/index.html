About us

About us – MeDišić family beekeeping farm

A home-grown family farm devoted to natural bee honey, pure acacia honey, and quality homemade fruit brandies.

Contact us Acacia honey

Our story

MeDišić is a home-grown family farm. We work together, in step with the season, and choose an approach that protects taste and trust — from hive to jar.

We believe good honey does not need to be “fixed”. That is why we insist on natural products and careful work with the bees.

Natural production

We are devoted to producing natural bee honey. Without rush, without shortcuts — with a clear focus on quality.

Every jar should reflect what the bees bring from the forage. That is why we choose a natural approach: pure honey, a clear character, and a taste you recognise.

We follow the same principle with our brandies — homemade production, clean fruit flavour, and patience in the process.

Carefully tended hives

We are especially devoted to pure acacia honey. It comes from carefully tended hives, in the season when acacia leaves its mark — light, mild, and distinctive.

Care for the bees is not an add-on to production. It is the foundation: healthy colonies, calm work, and honey that stays natural.

Our products

Natural honey and homemade brandies — from the same farm, to the same standard.

  • Natural acacia honey

    Pure acacia honey from carefully tended hives. A mild taste, a light colour, and a clear mark of natural production.

  • Homemade fruit brandies

    Alongside honey, we also produce quality homemade fruit brandies. We distill carefully, and our brandies have won awards in Serbia.
